ABOUT

Hi, I'm Rebecca from Fountain Valley, CA.

My interest lies in the interplay of how human behavior and cognition affect how one uses and experiences technology.

Welcome to my design portfolio! I am currently a Master of Information Science student studying Human-Computer Interaction at University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. I am absolutely fascinated by the relationship between human and technology. When I'm not designing things, I enjoy staying active, cooking, drawing and painting. I love going out to experience new things, meet people, and am constantly looking for more things to learn and aquire skills in.

During my time at UCI, I completed an undergrad honors thesis on the perception of gravity in a rotating virtual environment and studied the effects of resulting cybersickness from the use of virtual reality head mounted displays.
